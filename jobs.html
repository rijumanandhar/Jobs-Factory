How Do We Hire

To start your career with Job Factory, you need to be 18 or above. First, you need to Sign Up. After you've signed up you can apply for the jobs. While applying, there may be one or two job categories in which you find yourself capable of performing with quality. After going through all the job categories and selecting one, please send us your basic details through online application form or through email. You can find this in Application Procedure section of each job category.



After examining your job application, our expert recruitment panel may ask you to complete a sample assessment. Which you should complete in a time-bound interval and submit at a given link or via email. After submission of Assessments from your end, our Expert Panel escalates your job application to the next level. In some cases, multiple examinations take place to determine the genuinely/quality. After successfully appearing and passing all the above recruitment steps,you are asked to remain available on a specific date and time for the Live Interview. Usually, it takes place via Skype or Google Hangout.If the interview panel finds you suitable for the job category you had applied for, and If you could represent yourself the same way, you are entitled to get a call or letter from one of our Partner Employer. At the start of your journey with Job Factory, you get world-class support and best payout rates against your hardwork.
